The Aviation Side
Flying the Bombardier Global at the highest level

Connor flies the Bombardier Global XRS internationally for Solairus Aviation under Part 135. The Global XRS is an ultra-long-range large-cabin business jet built for nonstop transcontinental and transoceanic missions.

Beyond the flight deck, Connor serves on Solairus's safety committee — bringing a structured, systems-level approach to risk management that carries directly into how he vets charter operators for clients.

He is currently pursuing his Certified Aviation Manager (CAM) designation through NBAA — the gold standard credential for aviation management professionals.

The Travel Side
A travel advisor with a pilot's eye for detail

As a Fora-certified travel advisor with access to the Virtuoso network, Connor books luxury hotels and travel experiences for clients who expect more than a transactional booking experience.

Booking through Volare unlocks Virtuoso perks — room upgrades, daily breakfast, resort credits, and VIP treatment across 175,000+ properties — at no extra cost to you.

Connor's travel has taken him across Europe and beyond. His trip reports are first-hand accounts. When he recommends a hotel, he's been there — or knows the operator personally.
